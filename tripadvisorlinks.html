Thanks for nothing, Tripadvisor

Trying to scrape directly from Tripadvisor's site was not working at all, since they have a target="_blank" attribute on each of their links within the listings which opens the link in a new window. This would normally be fine, but the way that their pages are set up every time I tried to open one of these links it wouldn't properly open and would just advance to the next set of listings.

To circumvent this, I gave up on trying to scrape directly and instead adapted this python script to get an ouput of all of the links to the museum pages, then pasted them here and did my scraping from this page.
